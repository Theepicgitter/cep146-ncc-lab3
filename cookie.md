2. **Cream butter and sugars** together (butter should be softened)
3. **Add eggs and vanilla**, beat to combine
4. **Stir in dry ingredients** until just combined
5. **Add chocolate chips** and mix until evenly distributed
6. **Roll dough into balls** using a cookie scoop for uniform size
